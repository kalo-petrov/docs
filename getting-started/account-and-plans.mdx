## What counts as a Tracker Connection?

A connection is a saved API credential for one tracking platform account. If you have two separate Affise networks (e.g., one for brand A and one for brand B), that counts as two connections.

The Demo connection does **not** count against your connection limit.

## What counts as an Automation?

Any saved workflow in the Automations section — whether enabled or disabled — counts toward your limit. Deleting an automation frees up the slot.

## What counts as a Step?

Each action block inside an automation counts as a step. The step limit applies across **all** your automations combined, not per individual automation.

## What counts as a Run?

A run is one full execution of an automation — regardless of how many steps it contains. Runs reset on the first of each month.

## What counts as AI Usage?

Each AI operation consumes one credit:

- **AI Analyze step** executes → 1 credit
- **Generate Invoice Number step** executes → 1 credit
- **Generate with AI** button used in the email or invoice editor → 1 credit
- **AffZero AI chatbot** message sent → 1 credit

AI usage resets on the first of each month.

## Custom APIs

Custom API connections are available on the **Scale** plan at **$20 per API per month**. Each custom API counts separately toward your tracker connection limit.

## Viewing Your Usage

Go to **Settings → Usage** to see a real-time breakdown of:

- Automations and steps used vs. your limit
- Monthly runs consumed vs. your allowance
- Tracker connections in use
- AI credits consumed this month
- Your current plan and billing cycle

## Upgrading or Downgrading

Go to **Settings → Billing** to change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
