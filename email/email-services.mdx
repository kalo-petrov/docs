## Overview

AffZero sends emails from your automations using the email account you connect under **Providers → Gmail + GDrive**. No separate email service configuration is required.

Go to [Gmail & Google Drive](/providers/gmail-drive) to connect your Google account and enable email sending.

---

## Gmail (via Google OAuth)

When you connect a Google account in **Providers → Gmail + GDrive**, that address becomes available as a **Send From** option in all Send Email automation steps.

- Emails are sent from your actual Gmail or Google Workspace address
- Recipients see your real address in the From field
- Gmail's daily sending limits apply (~500/day for regular Gmail, 2,000/day for Google Workspace)
- You can connect multiple Google accounts depending on your plan

<Warning>
  If you revoke AffZero's access in your Google Account settings or your OAuth token expires, email sending will stop working for any automations using that account. Go to **Providers → Gmail + GDrive** and reconnect the account to restore access.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email not delivered">
    Check that your Google account hasn't revoked AffZero's access. Go to your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps and verify AffZero is listed. Also check Gmail's daily sending limits if you're sending high volumes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emails landing in spam">
    Emails sent via Gmail inherit your account's sending reputation. Make sure you're not sending to unverified lists, and avoid spam trigger words in subject lines. Consider using a Google Workspace account for better deliverability.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Send From' address not appearing in automation">
    Make sure the Google account is connected and verified in **Providers → Gmail + GDrive**. If the account shows as invalid, disconnect and reconnect it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
